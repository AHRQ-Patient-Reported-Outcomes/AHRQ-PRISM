Frequently Asked Questions
What does the PRISM app do?
The PRISM mobile app makes is easy for patients to take surveys and answer questions about how they are feeling. These “patient reported outcomes” can help inform your doctor about your condition in between office visits.
What is the PROMIS Physical Function survey?
This survey measures your capability for doing various physical activities. The app asks as few questions as needed to assess your overall physical abilities.
What does the score mean?
The score is a number between 1 and 100 that represents your physical function. The survey has been validated across a large population and the score is an indication of your physical function compared to other people of your gender and age group.
How will this information be used?
Your answers may be used by your doctor to help them understand how you are doing between office visits. Your doctor will be able to see trends as your treatment progresses.
